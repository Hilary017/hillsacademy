About Us

Our parents are appreciated and our students are valued. Babcock Academy is a people-oriented oriented institution. Your contribution will be highly valued and appreciated. We are agents of change with a social change agenda in support of the common man. We can do great things with your support of the common man. We can do great things with your support. The management team are all approachable and accessible to parents and pupils, and do encourage direct communication via e-mails and telephones.
