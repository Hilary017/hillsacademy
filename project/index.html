WELCOME TO HILLS ACADEMY

I am delighted to welcome you to Babcock Academy, a secondary school stablished by Babcock University Ilishan which is a Seventh Day Adventist Church Institution. Our school operates Adventist education which emphasize Holistic education Value based education Integrated education A journey to eternity Education which redeems and restores man to his original image ( Image of God) Promotion of spiritual, physical, mental and social development. Pursuance of academics to ensure excellent and wonderful results for higher education. Preparation of students for Joy filled lives for service to Human Kind Higher joy and under services in the world to come. Welcome to Babcock Academy – Light on the hill

WHAT WE OFFER


EXCELLENT FACILITIES

The school offers a world class education. We are in the process of building a school with every desirable facility to make a child’s six years stay an enviable and memorable experience. Comfortable boarding facilities, I.C.T., Library, Recovery Bay with experienced Nurses, Laboratories, Chapel, Cafeteria; Sport fields, etc are all available.

REASONABLE SCHOOL FEES

Our fees are affordable and reasonable. Excellent boarding facilities heaters, drinking water dispensers, treated boreholes and lots more.

WE VALUE EVERYONE

Our parents are appreciated and our students are valued. Babcock Academy is a people-oriented oriented institution. Your contribution will be highly valued and appreciated. We are agents of change with a social change agenda in support of the common man. We can do great things with your support of the common man. We can do great things with your support. The management team are all approachable and accessible to parents and pupils, and do encourage direct communication via e-mails and telephones. We invite you to join our campaign by choosing Babcock Academy.

MEDICAL CARE

A well-equipped recovery bay where students are looked after by competent, qualified health practitioners is put in place. Qualified nurses are available in case any child falls sick. Referral is available at Federal Medical Centre, Abeokuta or Babcock University Teaching Hospital, llishan-Remo.
